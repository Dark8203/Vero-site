How Vero lifts accuracy

Instrument your pipeline, generate ground truth, then iterate with evidence.

1) Instrument

Capture queries, contexts, and responses for every run. Support for LangChain/LlamaIndex, OpenAI/Azure, and vector DBs.

2) Evaluate

Measure retrieval (Precision/Recall/MRR), output quality (faithfulness, completeness), and latency/cost.

3) Improve

Compare prompts, retrievers, top‑k, chunking, and rerankers. Promote the variant that moves the metric — safely.
